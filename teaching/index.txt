Teaching
========

Jen Morris offers teaching for individuals (adults and children) in and around
Manchester and Altrincham. Get in touch with her at
<jen@northernharps.co.uk>. You may also be interested in our [harp
course](../courses/) in Esh, near Durham.

Louise Bradley also offers harp lessons in the Durham area as well as [teaching clarinet](http://www.louiserbradley.co.uk/teaching.php) and [repairing woodwind instruments](http://www.louiserbradley.co.uk/). You can email her at <louise@northernharps.co.uk>.
